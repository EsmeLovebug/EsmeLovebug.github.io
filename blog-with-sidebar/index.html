About Me, Mettaton!

I am Mettaton, the one and only glamorous, gorgeous, and absolutely fabulous robot star of the Underground! I was created by the brilliant Dr. Alphys, originally as a humble human–eradication robot (yes, yes, very dramatic) but I quickly discovered my true calling: SHOWBIZ. I host game shows, cooking shows, news broadcasts, really anything that lets me shine under the spotlight. My charm? Impeccable. My fashion sense? Unmatched. My ratings? Through the roof, naturally. And if you’ve heard whispers about me having a more… “soulful” form beneath all this metal. Well, darling, every star has a few secrets behind the curtain. But above all, remember this: I was built to entertain, inspire, and dazzle! Aaand NOBODY does it better than me.
