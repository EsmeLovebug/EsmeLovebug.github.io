Shepard's Pie

PREP TIME: 45 MINUTES
COOK TIME: 30 MINUTES
TOTAL TIME: 1 HOUR 15 MINUTES
YIELDS: 3 to 4

This shepherd’s pie is pure comfort in a dish with a rich, savory lamb filling and the creamiest mashed potatoes on top. Everything gets baked until golden with buttery, crispy edges that make it impossible to stop at one bite. It’s the kind of dinner that feels like a warm hug after a long day.
